Welcome to YZAK

At YZAK, we are driven by a commitment to community and service. We invite you to explore our mission and vision to see how together, we can make a lasting impact on the community and beyond. Here’s how we envision our role and our goals in fostering a culture of volunteerism and charity:
Our Mission:
 Represent the youth of Bahrain in the field of charitable contributions with the Bahrain trust foundation in all their various events and objectives.

 Our Vision: To become the leading youth team in the field of volunteering in the kingdom of Bahrain.
